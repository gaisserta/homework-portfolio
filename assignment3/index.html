Tom Swifties

Tom Swifities are phrases in which a quoted sentence is linked by a pun to the manner in which it is attributed.

  • “It’s been raining all day!” Tom yelled tempestuously.
  • “The lights are out,” Tom announced dimly.
  • “I’m on a yacht,” Tom declared sternly.
  • “My favorite subject is geometry,” Tom said squarely.
  • “I’m missing a ball,” Tom cried testily.
